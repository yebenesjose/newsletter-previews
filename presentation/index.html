Scenario 1 — Test the Waters

What You Get & What You Don't

✅ Gets

  • ● Basic email capture (logged-in users only)
  • ● One daily Connections hints email
  • ● Automated sending via Cloudflare Worker
  • ● Proper domain auth (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
  • ● Validates the concept: do users open & click?

❌ Missing

  • ● Guest capture (email input for non-logged-in)
  • ● Welcome emails / onboarding
  • ● Double opt-in compliance flow
  • ● Sync worker (Firestore ↔ SendGrid)
  • ● Unsubscribe management
  • ● Analytics / tracking
  • ● Re-engagement for inactive users

Risk Profile

Compliance (no double opt-in) Guest capture missing No sync = manual data management No engagement tracking Deliverability (domain auth included)

Bottom line: This is a proof-of-concept, not a product launch. Good for validating demand with minimal investment, but you cannot grow the list beyond logged-in users or measure success properly.
